## Problem
https://codeforces.com/contest/2149/problem/A

## Observation
To ensure that the product $a_1 \cdot a_2 \cdot ... \cdot a_N > 0$, is positive every zero must be removed. In addition, the number of 
-1 values must be even.

## Solution
$$
2 * (parity of the number negative ones) + (number of zeros)
$$
