### Problem
https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/1360/D
<details>
    <summary>Observations</summary>
The way to solve it is related to the factor of $N$
</details>
<details>
    <summary>Solution</summary>
Save all $x$ (factors of $N$ that less than $k$). Then, just check which one the minimum of $\frac{N}{k}$
</details>

### Time Complexity
$O(\sqrt{N})$
